# Metrics Vision: Time Investment & Focus Quality

This document outlines the metrics roadmap for Locked-In. These are designed for a philosophy of **Time Investment** (progressing in long-term topics) rather than simple task completion.

## 1. Focus Quality (The "Signal")
*   **Focus Ratio**: `(Active Work Seconds) / (Total Session Seconds)`. The primary health metric.
*   **Deep Work Blocks**: Count of work blocks >45 minutes. Measures the ability to reach flow.
*   **Fragmentation Index**: Ratio of tiny blocks (<5m) to long blocks. High fragmentation indicates "chopped up" focus.

## 2. Investment & Momentum (The "Long Game")
*   **Topic Momentum**: Cumulative hours spent per Tag/Topic over 7/30 days.
*   **The "Show Up" Rate**: % of days in a week where a specific high-priority topic was touched for at least 30 minutes.
*   **Context Concentration**: What % of your day went to your "Big 3" topics vs. miscellaneous overhead.

## 3. Flow vs. Friction (Analyzing Overextension)
*   **Flow Extension**: When a task is extended AND Focus Ratio remains >90%. (A performance "Win").
*   **Friction/Drag Extension**: When a task is extended BUT Interruption/Idle count is high. (Indicates procrastination or being stuck).

## 4. Interruption & Recovery (The "Cost")
*   **Interruption Density**: Number of interruptions (pauses/calls/idle) per focus hour.
*   **Recovery Velocity**: The time gap between `Pause/Idle Ended` and `Work Resumed`. Measures how quickly you "re-engage" with the topic.

## 5. Success & Consistency (The "Results")
*   **Best Day Score**: A composite score `(Focus Ratio × Total Focus Hours)`. Ranks your most effective historical days.
*   **Zero-Day Avoidance**: Tracking streaks where at least 1 focus block was recorded for a "Critical" long-term topic.
*   **Abandonment Patterns**: Identifying which topics are consistently avoided (e.g., "I always skip #writing on Fridays"). Helps identify friction in specific types of work.

## 6. Sustainability (The "Rhythm")
*   **Peak Progress Window**: Heatmap of when the highest Focus Ratios occur (e.g., "9 AM - 11 AM is the Sweet Spot").
*   **Break-to-Work Ratio**: Tracking the ratio of physical breaks (stretches/gym) to deep work to prevent burnout.
